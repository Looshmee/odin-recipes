Carbonara

Description

Prep time: 5 mins Cook time: 15 mins Total time: 20 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Guanciale
  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Cook the pork in olive oil until browned and crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  2. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Drain and return to the pot. Let cool.
  3. Whisk the eggs, 1/2 of the cheese, and some black pepper in a bowl until smooth.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy.
  5. Stir in the pork, then the remaining cheese and more black pepper.
